Currently overhauling website with ChatGPT. It's a really cool tool. But like any tool it requires a bit of skill to use effectively.

I think the key to getting meaningful results out of the AI tools is something that is already difficult enough to do in real life — asking the right questions! It requires an understanding of the problem at hand, an ability to break down the problem into chunks that you can tackle, and an ability to coherently communicate what that problem is. Tough stuff even before you tackle the AI specific details.
